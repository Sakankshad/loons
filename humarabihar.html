Who are we?

Humara Bihar is a social initiative by Loons along with a group of students hailing from Bihar and currently studying in reputed institutions of India. It also connects with other socially active members who want to contribute for the better representation of our state Bihar on different social platforms and work for the welfare of their homeland.

Originated in 2019, Humara Bihar actively works for the underprivileged and economically backward section of society and helps them to fulfill their basic needs like food, clothes and education. We have successfully executed 5 social events on ground that benefited more than 10,000 families directly or indirectly.

Flagship Events

Utsav

Utsav is the diwali celebration of Humara Bihar conducted for orphan kids. This celebration is dedicated to underprivileged children who don't have a family to share their happiness.

In Utsav, we come together and organise several fun sessions of art and craft, music and dance for children that gets concluded with a feast.

Hundreds of orphan children were helped with clothes, food and education on a long term basis through this initiative. Many people extended their support and came forward to celebrate, educate and feed the needy ones.

Sehyog

Sehyog is one of the biggest donation drives of Bihar. It works for economically backward families of the society and helps them with basic amenities like food, clothes and education to poor kids.

In Sehyog, the donation of unused books, clothes, toys and others are collected door to door from those who wish to donate and then distributed in major slum areas.

More than 5000 people have actively participated in the donation drive. We have successfully completed two editions of sehyog that has benefited 10,000 families.

Each One Feed One

Each one feed one is an initiative of Humara Bihar with a vision to eradicate hunger. It's a simple idea, If every financially sound person takes the responsibility to feeds one economically weak person, no one will ever sleep hungry.

We distribute food packet to people who are in dire need of it. This supports empathy and works on each others upliftment.

At the time of COVID-19 pandemic, most of the people were dying out of hunger and poverty. With Each One Feed One Campaign, we reached out to beggars, migrants and daily wage workers with food and hygiene kits and helped about 1000 families.
