House of
LoOns

The journey started when we were peddling on a freelancing project. We had ideas, vision and were bucked up to execute. Since, Creative Ideas could take you to any dimension but execution is the real game.

Hustling to bring the best work on table, we needed some creative artists who could bring colors to our project. And we started scanning all the places and lists.

We knocked on many doors and rode to many unpaved paths. The scanning made us realize that there’s an ocean of opportunities in our city. The budding city was and is in a transition phase of development and needs a lot of new creative ideas and technology to establish its infrastructure.

The dire demand of creative enthusiasts made us explore new artforms and meet many artists and developers. We realized that the polished artists or developers migrated due to lack of infrastructure and better job opportunities and the remaining one didn’t have projects or function on a haphazard platform for work and opportunities.

This brought us the idea of “LoOns” under which we wanted to establish a uniform platform for all the designers, developers and marketers who directly get associated with businesses and projects and could explore more opportunities and become an active contributor in the development of their homeland.

We planted a seed of our creatively driven integrated marketing agency named LoOns. Today, LoOns is actively functional and has transcended to 11 different industries. It has worked with more than 50 brands and paved its way to metropolitan cities as well.

How we do it

We all want to look attractive enough to catch everyone’s gaze. Afterall who doesn’t want to stand out? Same as our personality, Our businesses need representation too. We read, research and strategize to define the niche in the market and then groom businesses to brands with our multidimensional approach. This attracts consumer attention and helps to generate brand awareness in the established market.

Since the market is flooded with entrepreneurial ideas, information and services, It’s really difficult to have a definite marketing approach. Loons opens several gateways through one channel by adding three major prospects of sky-rocketing growth in today’s cut-throat competition. Our subtle way of untangling growth prospects are Creativity, Consultancy and Technology indeed like Arts, Commerce and Science.

Creativity

We develop and implement imaginative solutions to help businesses increase brand awareness, better designing and showcasing of products or services and, ultimately, increase profits. We use creativity to get our clients’ brands an unfair share of attention.

Consultancy

To read and reach the targeted audience, one must know the facts and figures. We focus on getting market insights, data, stats and probability to understand consumer behavior, strategize accordingly and then suggest the most responsive growth hacks.

Technology

Technology makes our lives way easier. It’s a potential tool that empowers businesses to reach a broad horizon of audience and turn them into potential customers. We use technology for evolving and sustaining business and make it more convenient and user-friendly.
